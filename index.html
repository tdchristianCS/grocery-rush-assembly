"Grocery Rush!" is a fast-paced game where the goal is to achieve the best Yelp reviews. Customers arrive at the store with various requests. Unfortunately, they are cats and can neither find items nor collect them for themselves, so you have to hand them the items yourself. When a customer receives an item, they leave a review. Customers have limited patience, and their review will be better depending on how quickly their request is fulfilled. If they run out of patience or are handed the wrong item, they leave a 1-star review. Some customers have no particular request. Also, the game gets harder over time.


Click on a bin to pick up an item. Then, click on a customer to hand them the item. If you want to get rid of the item you're holding, click on the trash bin. Press space to pause/unpause. P.S. To restart game, refresh page.
